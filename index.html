About LeafLens

Welcome to LeafLens, your ultimate plant image expert. LeafLens is a cutting-edge project designed to make plant identification and exploration effortlessly accessible. Simply upload an image of a plant, and our advanced machine learning model will work its magic, providing you with detailed information about the plant's species, characteristics, habitat, and care instructions. Whether you're a botany enthusiast, a student, or a gardening aficionado, LeafLens is your go-to resource for expanding your plant knowledge. Try LeafLens today and unlock the world of botanical discovery with ease.

How to Use

Using LeafLens is a breeze. Begin by uploading an image of the plant you want to identify, ensuring it's well-lit and captures the entire plant. Our advanced machine learning model will swiftly process the image, and within moments, you'll receive a comprehensive report detailing the plant's species, characteristics, habitat, and care instructions. Dive deeper by exploring related species and our extensive plant database. Share your findings with fellow enthusiasts, and don't hesitate to use LeafLens as your go-to tool for all your botanical inquiries. It's a user-friendly and educational experience, suitable for both novices and experts in the world of plants.
